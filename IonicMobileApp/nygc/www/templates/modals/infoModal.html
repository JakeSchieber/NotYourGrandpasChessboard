Info

  • Welcome to NYGC!

    This is the companion app to the NYGC autonomous chessboard. This app allows for you to connect your board to the game server and will give you updates regarding your game as it progresses. Make sure to have your app available throughout the entirety of your game!

    Turns: There will be a red/green label at the top of the information screen denoting whose turn it currently is. Only move pieces on the board when "You Turn" is present.

    Invalid Moves: Sometimes players make invalid moves or the board misses a step in your move pattern. If this happens an alert will pop up asking for you to reset the board to the last known board position. When you habe successfully reset the board to this piece layout the error message will go away.

  • Game in progress

    A current game is in progress. Would you like to restart the game or continue playing?

  • Game over!

    Bummer Dude, :'( You seem to have lost the game. Try again by hitting reset below.

    Congratulations! You have won! PANDA!!! Slay some more nerds by restarting the game below.
